ABOUT ME

Creativity is a natural extension of who you are, and I love expressing this visually. I am a graphic designer whos heart is in expressing my ideas visually and coming up with the best way to deliver a message. I am an aspiring graphic designer based in Johannesburg who finds interest in illustration, video editing, photography and animation. My strongest area of expertise is illustration, I have been using platforms such as adobe illustrator, adobe Photoshop as well as Clip Studio for 7 years. I am very passionate about art and design, my main inspirations come from Instagram artist, Disney animation and Japanese anime.

My goal and dream is to come up with visual solutions that will help build businesses across the country, as well as make my name known for the type of stylist artwork that I produce. I want to share the love and passion i have for art and design to everyone, even those who arent fascinated by are i hope that my art will be compelling to them therefore making them understand why art is so important.
